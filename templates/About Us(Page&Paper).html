Visit Our Bookshop

Founded in 2024, Page & Paper began as a small neighborhood bookstore driven by a deep love for literature and the written word. What started as a cozy corner filled with secondhand books soon grew into a vibrant community hub for readers, writers, and dreamers alike.

Over the year, Page & Paper has expanded its collection to include a wide range of genres — from timeless classics and modern bestsellers to local and independent works. The shop also hosts regular book readings, author meet-ups, and creative writing workshops, fostering a shared passion for storytelling.

Today, Page & Paper continues to honor its founding mission: to connect people through books and inspire a lifelong appreciation for reading.

Our Story

"Page & Paper" began with a simple love for stories — the kind that linger even after you've turned the last page. What started as a shared passion between readers soon grew into a space where books aren't just sold, but celebrated.

In an age where everything is digital and fleeting, Page & Paper was created to bring back the warmth of holding a story in your hands — the scent of paper, the weight of words, and the quiet joy of reading.

Every page tells a story, and every reader begins a journey. That's what Page & Paper stands for — a haven for those who find comfort, inspiration, and escape within the pages of a book.

Our Mission

At "Page & Paper", our mission is to connect readers with stories that inspire, comfort, and ignite imagination.

We strive to make books accessible and meaningful by supporting authors, nurturing a love for reading among all ages, and creating a warm, mindful online book website experience that feels as personal and inviting as a cozy reading nook.

Where every page truly begins a journey.
