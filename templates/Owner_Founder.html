Founder's Note

Page & Paper was founded by Recaña, Princes Khyle P., a 20-year-old student at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Sto. Tomas Campus (PUP-STC), pursuing a degree in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT).

Driven by a passion for storytelling and technology, she created Page & Paper to bring together readers and dreamers, combining love for literature with the creativity of digital design.

www.khylerecana.com

Founder's Personal Choice

One of my favorite literary works is The Odyssey by Homer. Its themes of perseverance, discovery, and longing for home deeply resonate with me. Much like Odysseus' journey, the creation of Page & Paper reflects a voyage — one driven by curiosity, growth, and an unending love for storytelling.

The song "Would You Fall in Love with Me Again" is a key part of the musical's story and is directly related to the book, as it is the final song that depicts the final reunion of Odysseus and Penelope in Homer's Odyssey. The song's lyrics and theme are based on this climactic moment in the epic poem where Odysseus must prove his identity to Penelope and she must decide if she can love him after his long absence and all the things he has done. This song is from the EPIC: The Musical. The musical is based on the ancient Greek epic poem The Odyssey by Homer, which is a book.
